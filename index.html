Supriya Bhide



Research Scholar, IIT Bombay
TCS Research Fellow

I am a PhD research scholar at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Bombay, where my research focuses on state-of-the-art intermediate representations for compilers. My work aims to improve compiler performance and efficiency, contributing to the advancement of this critical field. Beyond my research, I have a profound love for astronomy. The vastness of the universe fascinates me, and I often find myself lost in thought while gazing at the night sky, pondering the mysteries of the cosmos. In addition to my passion for astronomy, I enjoy singing, philosophical conversations, and exploring the world through activities like tinkering with machines, playing volleyball, and trekking. I also find joy in reading, constantly seeking new knowledge and perspectives.
